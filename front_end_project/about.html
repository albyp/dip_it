Our Story

In 2022, Ben Miller founded TK (Tek Knews), a blog company that quickly made a name for itself in the tech industry. With a strong background in Business and a deep passion for technology, Ben recognised the need for a platform that could bridge the gap between complex technological advancements and the general public. His vision was to create a space where tech enthusiasts and professionals alike could find accessible, insightful content that kept them informed about the latest developments in the tech world.

Ben's business acumen, combined with his love for technology, drove the growth of Tek Knews from a small blog into a recognized source for tech news and analysis. His approach was simple yet effective: provide high-quality, reliable content that resonates with readers. Under his leadership, TK has grown steadily, attracting a diverse audience and becoming a trusted name in tech journalism. Ben's commitment to excellence and innovation continues to guide the company as it expands its reach and influence in the digital landscape.

The team is made of like-minded authors that bring their own interests to their articles. Covering a wide range of themes and stories, we strive to ensure our material is enjoyable for all readers.
Our team are based all over the world, often travelling and keeping up-to-date with all the latest and greatest.
